The drawer has three states: closed, half and full. Use the ⟩⟩ / ⟨⟨ button to toggle between half and full, or the ✕ button to close it.

In half mode the first three items are shown. In full mode all six items appear in a two-column grid. If the viewport drops below 768 px while in full mode the drawer automatically collapses to half. When that collapses the active item out of view, the corresponding first-column item is selected instead.
